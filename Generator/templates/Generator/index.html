The Beats by (An)Drey Project

What is this?

The beats by (An)drey Markov project is an exploration into the randomness of music and lyrics. The back end consists of a multi-threaded web scraper that collects data from Genius.com and utilizes a markov chain to generate lyrics inspired by your favorite artists.
